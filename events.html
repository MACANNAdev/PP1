Foraging

Prepare to get your hands dirty! This workshop is all about being in nature, and learning about the habits and homes of our little(sometimes big!) fungi friends.

All the theory comes into practice now. You will be guided through my own process of finding mushrooms depending on the location we are in and the time of year.

Cultivation

Would you like to learn how to grow for yourself? Perhaps in your garden or even in a space inside.

This workshop is where we learn how to cultivate mushrooms from spores, liquid mycellium, agar and more. We will be working mostly inside in sterile conditions with various pieces of equipment. Time to get you mad scientist hat on!

Identification

I am available to give one hour presentations and talks to groups, associations and schools.

It is important to me to demysify our conceptions of mushrooms and to clearly outline where the dangers are so that we can then confidently engaging in foraging in a safe and joyful way

Cooking

So what to do with all these mushrooms? Mushrooms have been an integral part of our diet for millenia from japanese food to french cuisine and in modern times as a source of protein for vegans and vegetarians.

I am available to teach groups the best dishes I know and to give you pointers on finding out more on your own journey.
